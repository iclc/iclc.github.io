Biography

Tim Cowlishaw (London, 1983) is a researcher, designer, and technologist, currently enrolled in the PhD programme in the DARTS group at the Universitat Oberta de Catalunya, where he is investigating the materiality and ecology of digital waste, from a perspective that combines design research, STS, and the environmental humanities, as well as an experimental musician working with guitar, electronics and livecode. His work with livecode involves the manipulation of electro-acoustic signals in real-time, and the invention of instruments and effects to be used alongside other instruments. He has presented work at VIU and Eufònic festivals, Sónar+D, CCCB, Medialab Matadero and the Museum of the Moving Image, NYC.
