Program Notes

The Printer Jam (Project Cassiel X BITPRINT) is a performance duo using livecoding to take advantage of the intrinsic musicality of 3D printing. The performers create 3D printed objects that, during fabrication, activate the printer motors at certain musical pitches and rhythms which are then recorded, processed and looped, building up both physical objects and rhythmic soundscapes. Each performance and its resulting artefacts are unique experiences. This work “entangles the artist, machine, algorithms, and their potential audience in the creation and re-creation of the work, questioning the finality and determinacy of controlled fabrication processes” [Raskob, 178:2023]
